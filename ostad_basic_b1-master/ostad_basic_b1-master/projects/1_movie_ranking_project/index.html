The Best Movies According to Faisal Ahmed

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972) directed by Francis Ford Coppola

The Godfather is a classic American crime film that tells the story of the Corleone family and their involvement in the mafia underworld. Starring Marlon Brando and Al Pacino, the film has won multiple Academy Awards and is considered one of the greatest films of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) directed by Frank Darabont

Based on a novella by Stephen King, The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of two men serving life sentences in prison and their bond over the years. Starring Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman, the film is widely regarded as one of the greatest films ever made, and has a loyal fan base.

The Godfather: Part II (1974) directed by Francis Ford Coppola

The Godfather: Part II is a sequel to The Godfather, and is often considered one of the few sequels that surpasses the original. The film follows the rise of Vito Corleone and the struggles of his son, Michael Corleone. Starring Al Pacino,Robert De Niro, and Diane Keaton, the film has won multiple Academy Awards and is widely regarded as one of the greatest films ever made.
